Overview

Welcome to Solar Cleaning Expert. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App") available on Android.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Data is only used to operate, improve, and support the App and the services you book through it.
🗂️

How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: Create and manage your account, process bookings and subscriptions, dispatch service partners.
  • Payments: Initiate and verify payments through Razorpay.
  • Notifications: Send booking confirmations, service reminders, and promotional updates via push notifications and in-app alerts.
  • Support: Respond to your queries and resolve complaints.
  • Improvement: Analyse usage patterns to improve features, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and protect user accounts.
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfil obligations under applicable laws and regulations.
🤝

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service Partners: Your name, phone number, and service address are shared with the assigned cleaning technician solely to complete your booking.
  • Payment Processor: Booking amounts are passed to Razorpay to process your payment. Razorpay operates under its own privacy policy.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication, cloud storage, and push notifications.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred; you will be notified in advance.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your use of the App.
  • Account Deletion: Upon deletion, personal data is permanently removed or anonymised within 30 days.
  • Payment Records: Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required under Indian financial laws (GST, IT Act).
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 12 months to resolve follow-up queries.
🛡️

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain/keystore.
  • Firebase App Check prevents unauthorised API access.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by PCI-DSS compliant Razorpay – we never see your card details.
  • Account deletion requires password confirmation to prevent unauthorised deletion.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please keep your login credentials confidential.

👶

Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete profile information directly in the App.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated personal data (see below).
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke optional permissions (location, notifications) through device settings.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format by contacting us.
  • Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us; we will respond within 30 days.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@solarcleaningexpert.co.in.

🗑️

Account & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account inside the App:

  1. Open the App and log in.
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) to open the side drawer.
  3. Tap "Delete Account" (shown in red).
  4. Read the confirmation warning and tap Continue.
  5. Enter your password to confirm.
  6. Your account and data will be permanently deleted.
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Active subscriptions, booking history, and all personal data will be removed within 30 days. Payment transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by law.

Prefer to request deletion by email?

If you are unable to access the App, send a deletion request from your registered email address.
