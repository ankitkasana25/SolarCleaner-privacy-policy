Option 1 — Delete Directly Inside the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the Solar Cleaning Expert app:

  1. 1
    Open the App Launch Solar Cleaning Expert on your Android device and log in.
  2. 2
    Open the Side Drawer Tap the menu icon ☰ at the top-left corner of the screen.
  3. 3
    Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom of the menu and tap Delete Account (shown in red).
  4. 4
    Read the Warning & Confirm A dialog will list what will be deleted. Tap Continue to proceed.
  5. 5
    Enter Your Password Confirm your identity by entering your account password. Then tap Delete.
  6. 6
    Done Your account is deleted immediately. All personal data is removed within 30 days.
